AI-Powered Learning Platform Optimization

Scaling educational technology through user research and data-driven product strategy

Role
Product Manager

The Challenge

Columbia Build Lab's AI-powered transcription platform was experiencing low user engagement despite serving educational content to 1,000+ weekly users. Students were not completing courses, session times were short, and the platform struggled to retain users beyond initial signup. The challenge was to identify why users weren't engaging and develop a systematic approach to improve platform stickiness while supporting the underlying AI transcription technology.

My Strategic Approach

User Research & Data Analysis

Conducted 15+ structured user interviews across 3 distinct customer segments (undergraduate students, graduate students, and working professionals). Analyzed user behavior data using SQL queries and Python to identify drop-off points and usage patterns.

A/B Testing Framework Implementation

Designed and executed systematic A/B testing protocols with rigorous statistical significance testing. Created hypothesis-driven experiments for UI improvements, content organization, and user onboarding flows.

Analytics Infrastructure Development

Built comprehensive analytics infrastructure using database queries and API integration. Created real-time decision frameworks for cross-functional teams and established data quality standards that improved platform reliability.

Cross-Functional Execution

Led systematic process improvements and quality assurance protocols, mentoring junior developers on best practices. Coordinated with engineering, design, and content teams to ensure aligned execution.

Key Results & Impact

22% User Engagement Improvement
2 Sprints Reduced in Dev Cycles
1,000+ Weekly Active Users Served
10x User Growth Projections Supported

Technical Implementation

Self-directed learning of Django and AWS architecture enabled rapid prototyping and deployment of customer-focused features. Implemented ML-based speech recognition APIs and statistical significance testing frameworks to optimize user flows through data-driven product iterations. The platform now scales to support 10x user growth projections with improved reliability and performance.

Business Impact & Learnings

Strategic Value: This project demonstrated how user-centered product development combined with technical implementation can drive measurable business outcomes. The 22% engagement improvement translated to increased user retention and course completion rates.

Key Insights: The most impactful changes came from understanding user workflow rather than adding features. Simple UX improvements and personalized content recommendations had greater impact than complex technical enhancements.

What I'd Do Differently: Earlier integration of user feedback loops and more granular cohort analysis to understand long-term user behavior patterns.

Strategic Challenge

Financial due diligence typically requires 40+ hours of manual document review per deal, with analysts sifting through 10-K/Q filings, earnings reports, and unstructured financial documents. This manual process is prone to errors, inconsistent across analysts, and creates bottlenecks in investment decision-making. The goal was to build an AI system that could automate this analysis while maintaining accuracy and providing transparent source attribution.

Technical Architecture & Business Strategy

End-to-End RAG Pipeline Development

Constructed comprehensive RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) pipeline using GPT-4, LlamaIndex, and Elasticsearch. Designed system architecture to process both structured (SQL databases) and unstructured (PDF, 10-K/Q filings) financial data sources.

Custom ML Model Fine-Tuning

Fine-tuned RoBERTa-based fact-checker using Hugging Face Transformers, achieving 87% F1 score on custom financial claim datasets. Developed specialized models for financial entity recognition and sentiment analysis.

Citation-Aware QA System

Engineered NodeWithScore functionality to surface inline references alongside retrieved document answers. Built ETL pipelines with PyPDF2 and custom parsers to maintain document lineage and ensure audit trails.

Scalable Content Intelligence

Created platform capable of processing large-scale financial datasets with inline source attribution, enabling analysts to verify AI-generated insights against original documents.

Business Impact & Results

87% Citation-Verified Accuracy
10-K/Q Automated Filing Analysis
40+ Hours Saved Per Deal
100% Source Attribution

Strategic Value & Market Application

Industry Transformation: This platform addresses a critical pain point in financial services where speed and accuracy of due diligence directly impact deal flow and investment returns. The ability to maintain 87% accuracy while providing full source attribution meets regulatory requirements for investment decision documentation.

Competitive Advantage: Unlike existing solutions that provide black-box analysis, this system offers transparency through citation-aware responses, enabling analysts to verify and build upon AI-generated insights.

Scalability Considerations: The modular architecture supports expansion to additional document types (merger agreements, credit reports) and integration with existing investment workflows.

DC Optimal Power Flow: Strategic Grid Optimization

Mathematical optimization and risk modeling for large-scale energy systems

Complex Systems Challenge

Energy grid operators face the challenge of minimizing operational costs while maintaining system reliability under uncertainty. With renewable energy integration creating volatility in supply, traditional optimization methods struggle to balance cost efficiency with risk management. This project addressed optimizing a 1,814-bus power network with 395 generators while accounting for stochastic wind generation patterns.

Strategic Optimization Approach

Mathematical Model Formulation

Developed DC-OPF optimization model using piecewise-linear generation cost functions in Python. Implemented system-wide constraints for generation limits, power balance equations, and transmission capacity while penalizing load shedding scenarios.

Stochastic Risk Modeling

Simulated 1,000 wind generation scenarios using multivariate Gaussian models to capture uncertainty in renewable energy output. Applied Monte Carlo methods to assess financial risk and operational stability.

Value-at-Risk Analysis

Estimated financial risk exposure using Value-at-Risk (VaR) methodology, quantifying potential losses at $756K under adverse conditions. This provided decision-makers with clear risk metrics for strategic planning.

Bottleneck Analysis & Optimization

Conducted systematic bottleneck analysis across the 395-generator network, identifying capacity constraints and optimization opportunities that enabled 25.5% cost reduction.

Quantified Business Impact

25.5% Operational Cost Reduction
$756K Risk Exposure Quantified
1,000 Stochastic Scenarios Analyzed
395 Generators Optimized

Strategic Implications for Energy Sector

Industry Application: This optimization framework directly addresses grid operators' need to balance cost efficiency with reliability as renewable energy penetration increases. The 25.5% cost reduction demonstrates significant potential for operational savings across the energy sector.

Risk Management Framework: The VaR analysis provides a systematic approach to quantifying operational risk under uncertainty, enabling better capital allocation and strategic planning decisions.

Scalability & Policy Implications: The methodology scales to larger networks and supports policy analysis for renewable energy integration, transmission planning, and market design decisions.
