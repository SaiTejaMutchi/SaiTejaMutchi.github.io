AI-Powered Learning Platform Optimization

Scaling educational technology through user research and data-driven product strategy

Timeline
Jan - May 2025
Role
Product Manager
Users Served
1,000+ weekly
Team Size
5 people

The Challenge

Columbia Build Lab's AI-powered transcription platform was experiencing low user engagement despite serving educational content to 1,000+ weekly users. Students were not completing courses, session times were short, and the platform struggled to retain users beyond initial signup. The challenge was to identify why users weren't engaging and develop a systematic approach to improve platform stickiness while supporting the underlying AI transcription technology.

My Strategic Approach

User Research & Data Analysis

Conducted 15+ structured user interviews across 3 distinct customer segments (undergraduate students, graduate students, and working professionals). Analyzed user behavior data using SQL queries and Python to identify drop-off points and usage patterns.

A/B Testing Framework Implementation

Designed and executed systematic A/B testing protocols with rigorous statistical significance testing. Created hypothesis-driven experiments for UI improvements, content organization, and user onboarding flows.
