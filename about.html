My Journey

I began in a small Indian town, where my fascination with engineering was sparked by CAD tools in my father's workshop. That curiosity led me to the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), where I ranked in the top 1% of over a million applicants.

After graduation, I joined Dassault Systèmes as an R&D engineer. Over three years, I led development of high-impact CAD tools, migrated legacy software to the web, and improved machine learning based measurement systems by 25%.

Wanting to merge technical expertise with strategic thinking, I transitioned to Columbia Business School. As an MSBA candidate, I've served as a course assistant, led AI research, and built a GPT-powered investment intelligence platform.

From IIT to Columbia, from code to product, my path reflects a single theme: taking bold steps to solve complex problems at scale.
